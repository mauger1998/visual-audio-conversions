HOW IT WORKS

1

The first step is to drop us a line via the contacts page. We will get back to you to determine the type of media you have for conversion and quantity. We will also give you an idea of price and timeframe.

2

Once you have decided to go ahead, either deliver or post your media to us making sure to include your name and contact details.

3

We will confirm safe receipt of your order, detailing what we have received, whether any repair or additional procedures such as mould removal will be required.

4

Once everything is confirmed we will set to work on your order as soon as possible using our top of the range equipment

5

As soon as your order is ready we will get back in touch to arrange a collection/shipping date & send across your invoice. Payment can be made via bank transfer or cash.
