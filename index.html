CONVERT ANY FORMAT TO DIGITAL

We use the latest, top of the range equipment to convert almost every audio visual format to digital. Want to know more? Click on the icons below to read more about some of the formats we specialise in.

Audio Cassette

Everyone's seen an audio cassette, but did you know they come in several shapes and sizes? Standard "Compact Cassette", "Mini" Audio Cassette, "Micro" Audio Cassette and they aren't just for audio, they also store data!

Whether it's a personal mix tape, dictaphone recordings, answerphone messages... whatever type of Audio Cassette you have, we can help transfer your precious memories to digital.

We recommend supplying them to you in high-definition WAV files, but we can convert them to almost every known audio format including MP3.

Betamax

Like VHS and other delicate tapes, Betamax tapes are fragile. Don’t leave it to chance, get your precious memories converted to digital, safe in the knowledge that we use the very best equipment that is regularly serviced to ensure you always receive the best possible conversion.

If you have broken or damaged tapes don’t panic, we carry out a wide range of repair work from simple tape splicing or mechanism servicing to total cassette housing replacement.

Camcorder Tapes

Now camcorder tapes come in lots of different shapes, sizes and formats! We take the hassle out of converting the footage from all of these formats - simply send us a photo of your collection using the “contact us” page and we will let you know which formats you have and give you an idea of price.

  • Video8
  • Hi8
  • Digital8
  • MiniDV
  • SVHS-C
  • Betacam and many more.

Cine Film

When it comes to Cine Film conversions, not all conversions are equal. We often convert VHS or Betamax tapes of previously converted Cine Film and cringe at the quality.

Thankfully technology has advanced significantly over the past few decades, and we are now able to truly bring your old Cine Film back to life. We convert both Standard8 and Super8 films with or without audio to high definition MP4.

DAT Tapes

A little-known fact about DAT Tapes (Digital Audio Tapes) is that they were also used for data storage.

Whether you have Audio DAT Tapes or Data DAT Tapes, we can convert them using our mix of top-spec hardware and software to give you rich, studio quality 24bit 96kHz digital audio copies or bit-for-bit copies of your precious data.

We recommend supplying them to you in high-definition WAV files but we can convert them to almost every known audio format.

DVD | CD

No no no no one one one one wants to hear the sound of their treasured CD skipping but unfortunately CDs are very prone to scratching. If the worst was to happen, we use specialist data recovery software to recover your precious audio / video.

Better still, why not have your CD / DVD collection converted to digital before they get scratched? We convert all formats of CDs and DVDs (including mini-DVD) and we can supply the digitised copies in virtually any format under the sun!

LP

There’s just something about Vinyl!

The trouble is, vinyl is fragile and record players don’t fit in your pocket very easily, let alone your record collection.

We use top of the range pre-amps to bring out the very best, rich sound of vinyl, recording to studio quality 24bit 96kHz WAV or MP3.

Mini Disk

We often talk about degradation – it’s heart-breaking to see our customer’s treasured video collection covered in mould or delaminating due to age.

Thankfully we are able to successfully recover most footage BUT, luckily, MiniDisc doesn’t suffer with these sorts of issues and is probably one of the most reliable storage mediums as far as longevity is concerned. Chances are they will outlive us! Get in touch to find out how we can convert your MiniDisc collection to High Definition (studio quality 24bit 96kHz, no less!) WAV or MP3.

Phone | Tablet

When was the last time you checked how many photos or videos you have on your phone?

Are they backed up? Can you transfer them to your new phone? Can you easily share them with friends and family?

Wouldn’t it be great if there was a quick, simple service which enabled you to transfer the best of your photos and videos to USB, DVD or secure link to save for safe keeping or share with family and friends.. well, now there is!

Reel to Reel

Like Vinyl, there’s just something about Reel to Reel tape that newer format’s simply can’t replicate! Unfortunately, even the latest Reel to Reel players are very old now and unless you have your player regularly serviced your tapes are at risk if you run them through a faulty player.

Don’t leave it to chance, get in touch today to see how we can digitise your reels to studio quality 24bit 96kHz WAV or MP3 (or almost any format you like!) We convert all reel sizes up to 10.5” both in standard and (professional) high speed.

Slides, Negatives and Prints

Do you have family albums trapped on slides or negatives? Perhaps you have a single hardcopy album that you would like to share with family or friends? Or safeguard for future generations?

Like many formats, slides, negative and prints etc. deteriorate over time, and the more they deteriorate, the harder it is to restore them. There literally is no better time than the present (except yesterday..) to have them digitised. Our top of the range equipment creates high-definition digital copies that we can supply to you on USB, DVD or secure link. We can even restore colour and correct blemishes too.

VHS

Who doesn’t have cringy home videos stranded on old VHS tapes boxed up in the loft? Show those tapes the light of day again and get in touch today to find out how we convert them to USB, DVD or Digital Transfer for safe keeping or sharing with friends and family.

VHS Tapes - like many camcorder tapes, can significantly deteriorate over time. Most of the time we are able to recover your tapes to a very high standard but the longer they are left, the more difficult it becomes and sometimes tapes can deteriorate so badly that very little or even nothing can be recovered. This is your time to act! We use the very best professional spec Panasonic converters to digitise your tapes at the very best quality. VHS, S-VHS, VHS-C, PAL and NTSC.. we convert them all!
